CAD MODEL

3D model based on "Kuzco's Poison" from Emperor's New Groove, modeled in TinkerCad


About:

For this project, I used the online 3D-modeling software TinkerCad to make an iconic item from one of my favorite movies. Since it was printed in one color, I inset the llama image as an "engraving" on the bottle. The bottle was not printed to scale, but is my own tiny recreation of the animated prop.
